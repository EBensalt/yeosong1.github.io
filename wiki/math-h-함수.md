fabs(double);

~~~

~~~
sqrt(double);
~~~

## cube root (세제곱근)
~~~

## power (거듭제곱)
~~~
pow(double x, double y);

예시
pow(2,3); ---> 8
~~~

~~~
hypot(double 변1, double 변2);
~~~

## rounds (반올림)
~~~
nearbyint(double x);            round(double x);
nearbyintf(float x);            roundf(float x);
nearbyintl(long double x);      roundl(long double x);

가장 가까운 정수 리턴
1.4 -> 1
1.5 -> 2
~~~
